Ocean Acidification

What is Ocean Acidification?

Ocean Acidification is the process in which Carbonic Acid concentration is built up in our oceans. This drops the pH of our oceans and leads to massive loss of life in our oceans. Much of the coral reefs of the world are irreversibly lost and the creatures in our oceans are suffering from it. This in turn affects humans by reducing food supplies, damaging economies and lowering life diversity in the ocean to the point where many are headed toward extinction.

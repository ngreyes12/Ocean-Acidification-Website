Solutions

Electric

While there are many ways to combat carbon emissions, a huge step forward that many places are taking is the slow removal of gas powered cars. For example, the state of California is only allowing vehicles with zero emissions to be sold be 2035.

Nuclear Energy

Nuclear energy has proven to be much cleaner than other non-renewable sources of energy. Much of the world is still relying on fossil fuels for energy and it needs to stop. While renewable power is the main goal, it is important for countries to use nuclear energy to help them move toward completely renewable sources.

Renewable Energy

There are new technologies we can take advantage of and improve on to expand renewable energy. Solar, wind, hydraulic, and more all offer alternative energy sources with little to no carbon emissions.

Tree Regrowth

If our world is to stand a chance at stopping or maybe reversing some effects of global warming we need our trees back. We have lost more than half of the tree life in the world and we need to replant until the population of trees can reverse the amount of Carbon Dioxide we are releasing.
